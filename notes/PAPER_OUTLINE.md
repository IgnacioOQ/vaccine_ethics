## Working thesis

Broad vaccination is not unconditionally optimal: under evolutionary viral
dynamics, "Vaccinate All" can increase total harm relative to "Vaccinate
Vulnerable Only" in identifiable parameter regions. A precautionary (minimax)
analysis clarifies when each strategy is the safer choice.
